SalaryLens Privacy Policy SalaryLens does not collect, store off your device, transmit, or sell any personal data. All salary calculations happen locally in your browser. The extension uses Chrome's local storage only to remember the values and offers you last entered, on your own device. The only network request SalaryLens ever makes is to verify a Pro license key: if you buy Pro and enter your license key, that key is sent to Gumroad (api.gumroad.com), our payment provider, to confirm your purchase is valid. No salary data, browsing data, or personal information is sent. If you never buy Pro, the extension makes no network requests at all. Removing the extension deletes all locally stored data.
